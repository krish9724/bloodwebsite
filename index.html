Eligibility Requirements

You Can Donate If You:

  • Are between 18-65 years old
  • Weigh at least 110 pounds
  • Are in good general health

You Cannot Donate If You:

  • Have a cold or flu
  • Recently had surgery
  • Have certain medical conditions
